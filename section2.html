Planning stage

Here's where you'll find how to write your rough copy.

Writing tips

Remember that your writing needs to be engaging for the reader, and especially very interesting. Take a look at Figures of Speech, those are very helpful when you want to make a good story.

Adding lots of emotion and very detailed writing is a good way to make the reader feel and connect to the charcaters or the thing experiencing the event. Using high-level words is another way of making the book sound proffesional. Make the beginning of the book enriching, so the reader is very interested, the middle having lots of important details and gripping happenings, and the ending having a conclusion. Do not simply end it with no meaning, make sure the book has a problem that was solved at the end.

Making the rough copy

When you start writing out your rough copy, you can do it on paper, but it is much easier to do it on a Word Document or Google Docs. Use "Writing tips" above to help you.
