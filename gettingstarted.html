Get Started

Let's get started and release all that creativity!

Making the good copy

Woah, you're story's looking great! What if you made a good copy and finished it off?First, check everything and look for errors and anything you want to change. If you have a long chapter book, review each chapter one by one and take your time. It will be worth it, so that in the end you aren't ashsamed of all those little, silly mistakes.

Finishing up

Great job, you've finished your book! If you can, get a somebody, a friend, parent, or teacher, and let them read your book. Once they approve, double-check it just ONE MORE TIME. And when you've done that, you can sit back and admire your amazing book.
